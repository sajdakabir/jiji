Jiji

Track your claude.ai usage from your macOS menu bar.

Download for macOS

What it does

  • Live percentage of your current claude.ai session
  • Weekly usage for all models and Sonnet only
  • Reset times so you know when your quota refreshes
  • Native macOS menu bar app — no Dock icon, no fuss

Install

  1. Download the .dmg above
  2. Open it, drag Jiji.app into Applications
  3. Launch Jiji from Launchpad or Spotlight
  4. Sign in to claude.ai when the login window appears

Requirements

macOS 13 (Ventura) or newer, an active claude.ai login.
