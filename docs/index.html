What it shows

  • Weekly usage — all models & Sonnet only
  • Reset times for every limit
  • Sign in once; your session stays on your Mac

Install

  1. Download the .dmg and open it
  2. Drag Jiji into Applications
  3. First launch: right-click → Open
  4. Sign in to claude.ai when prompted
